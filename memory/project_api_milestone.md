**Why:** BioIntel Explorer aggregates data from 89+ free public APIs, providing comprehensive molecule profiles without any paid services.

**How to apply:** Use this as reference for:
- API categories and their sources
- Adding new free APIs (verify they're free before integrating)
- Understanding data coverage

**API Categories:**

- openFDA, Orange Book, RxNorm, DailyMed, ATC, NDC, NADAC, DrugBank, DrugCentral, PharmGKB, CPIC, RepoDB, DrugAge, ChemSpider

### Clinical & Safety (10 APIs)
- ClinicalTrials.gov, ISRCTN, Adverse Events, Recalls, ChEMBL Indications, ClinVar, GWAS Catalog, ToxCast, SIDER, IRIS

### Molecular & Chemical (15 APIs)
- PubChem Properties, PubChem Hazards, ChEBI, CompTox, KEGG, Rhea, Metabolomics, MyChem, HMDB, MassBank, MetaboLights, GNPS, BioCyc, SMPDB

### Bioactivity & Targets (10 APIs)
- ChEMBL, BioAssay, ChEMBL Mechanisms, IUPHAR, BindingDB, Pharos, DGIdb, Open Targets, CTD, IEDB

### Protein & Structure (13 APIs)
- UniProt, InterPro, EBI Proteins, Protein Atlas, QuickGO, PDB, PDBe Ligands, AlphaFold, PeptideAtlas, PRIDE, CATH/Gene3D, ModBase, SAbDab

### Genomics & Disease (15 APIs)
- NCBI Gene, Ensembl, Expression Atlas, GEO, dbSNP, ClinGen, MedGen, Monarch, NCI Thesaurus, MeSH, DisGeNET, Orphanet, MyGene, Bgee, OMIM

### Interactions & Pathways (10 APIs)

### Research & Literature (9 APIs)
- NIH RePORTER, PatentsView, SEC EDGAR, Europe PMC, PubMed, Semantic Scholar, OpenAlex, OpenCitations, CrossRef, arXiv

**Policy:** Only free public APIs. No paid services.
